Live the Experience

Come and live a unique experience with the Lafkenche culture on the beautiful Cantapiedra beach.

You will be able to participate in workshops on Mapuche handicrafts, medicinal uses of plants, and traditional cooking, where you will learn from the local community. Enjoy a lunch with authentic flavors and explore breathtaking landscapes while connecting with nature.

Sustainability

This experience is made with respect for the environment. The community's ruka-style cabins are also eco-friendly, designed to blend into the landscape and minimize ecological impact. Activities are designed to honor Lafkenche traditions while supporting sustainable practices that protect local resources.

Hospitality

Our community hosts warmly welcome each guest, offering a journey of curiosity and appreciation for the Mapuche Lafkenche culture. From traditional workshops to meals with authentic flavors, every detail is carefully prepared to make you feel at home and connected to the community.

Safety Measures

  • Cultural Respect: Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Respect for Nature: Do not pick plants or alter the natural environment.
  • Local Guides: All activities will be conducted by trained guides.
  • Medical Planning: Inform guides of any previous medical conditions.
  • First Aid: Guides are trained in first aid.
  • Equipment: Use the safety equipment provided.
  • Weather: Dress appropriately according to weather conditions.
  • Hydration: Stay hydrated, especially during outdoor activities.
  • Wildlife: Observe wildlife from a safe distance; do not feed animals.
  • Avoid Risks: Do not approach dangerous zones or restricted areas.
