About Us

At Cultural Rides & Wellness, we create wellness and accessible tourism experiences. Immerse yourself in nature and culture while enjoying therapeutic and ethno-touristic experiences that revitalize body and mind.

Mission

To offer inclusive tourism experiences that connect people with nature, culture, and wellness.

Vision

To transform the way people experience tourism by creating inclusive environments for everyone, regardless of their abilities.

Services

Equine Therapy

Therapeutic equestrian activities adapted for all abilities, promoting physical and emotional well-being.

Trekking and Photography

Guided tours to explore the beach and biodiversity, with picnic areas and bird-watching spots.

Lafkenche Cultural Tourism

Connection with the Lafkenche culture through craft workshops, gastronomic routes, and river navigation.
